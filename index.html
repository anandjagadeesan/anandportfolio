About Me

I'm Anand Jagadeesan, Based in India.

Anand Jagadeesan, a seasoned professional, embarked on his career as a Graphic artist, showcasing innate visual expression. With a talent for building client relationships and fast learning, he transitioned into Sales, Marketing, and Business development in the Media & Entertainment industry.

Anand's early career spanned into IT Products, Software Training, and significant contributions to Photography and Motion Pictures, working with industry giants like Kodak and FujiFilm. Navigating the analog to digital shift, he gained unique insights into visual technologies' evolving landscape. Today, Anand excels in the Vfx and CGI Industry, blending technical expertise with creative flair for a seamless visual storytelling dance. Explore this site for more on his contributions.
